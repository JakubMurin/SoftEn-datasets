1. Researcher logs into the DMPTool.
2. Researcher navigates to the project DMP.
3. DMPTool fetches DMP record details.
4. Researcher selects "Add Publication" in DMPTool.
5. Researcher enters publication details into DMPTool.
6. DMPTool checks if the publication is in the Repository.
7. DMPTool links publication with DMP record.
8. Researcher requests research output report from DMPTool.
9. DMPTool fetches all publications linked to DMP from the Repository.
10. Reporting Module generates a report based on the publications.
11. DMPTool displays/downloads the generated report for Researcher.
