1. User configures a house.
2. MainUI requests data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) from HouseConfigurationUI.
3. User inserts data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) into HouseConfigurationUI.
4. HouseConfigurationUI sets the house.
5. HouseConfigurationController reconfigures the house.
6. House validates the data.
7. House displays the configured house to the User.
