1. Business User registers as a listener for Fault Management Job and Report notifications in the Hub.
2. Business User creates a Fault Management Job in the SOF:FaultManagement system.
3. Fault Management Job is created successfully with a response sent to the Business User.
4. Fault Management Job generates necessary data.
5. Reporting period elapses.
6. Fault Management Report is created based on the Job.
7. Fault Management Report collection process starts.
8. Fault Management Report data is collected and report is marked as completed.
9. Report ready event is triggered as scheduled elapses.
10. Fault Management Job is marked as completed or scheduled.
11. Business User requests to get the Report from SOF:FaultManagement system.
12. Fault Management Report is retrieved from the system and response is sent to the Business User.
