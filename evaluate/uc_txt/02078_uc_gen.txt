1. Actor connects to Bootstrap.
2. Bootstrap validates connection.
3. Bootstrap resolves and establishes connection.
4. Bootstrap initializes and registers components.
5. Bootstrap initializes channels and sets up handlers.
6. Bootstrap sets channel options and attributes.
7. Bootstrap registers with event loop.
8. Bootstrap registers with channel and completes registration.
9. Async return process with Future.
10. Bootstrap continues resolve and connect process.
11. Bootstrap connects to channel.
12. Channel performs connection and triggering connect events.
13. Channel completes connection and activates channel.
