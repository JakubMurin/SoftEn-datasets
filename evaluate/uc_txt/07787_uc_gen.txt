1. Apelant inserts the money.
2. 2 minutes countdown occurs.
3. Apelant composes the number.
4. If the number is not composed - Apelant hangs up.
5. If the number is composed - Connects with Correspondant.
6. If the line is occupied - Apelant hangs up.
7. If the line is free - Correspondant picks up.
8. Consumption of money loop: money is counted and added.
9. Apelant and Correspondant communicate.
10. Correspondant hangs up.
11. Apelant hangs up.
