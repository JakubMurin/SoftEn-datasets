1. Counselor logs in to the Camp Platform.
2. Platform fetches Counselor's assigned campers from the Camp Database.
3. Platform lists the assigned campers for the Counselor.
4. Counselor selects "Take Attendance" option.
5. For each camper, Counselor marks the attendance.
6. Platform updates the attendance of the camper in the Database.
7. Platform saves the attendance records in the Database.
8. Platform displays the final confirmation to the Counselor.
