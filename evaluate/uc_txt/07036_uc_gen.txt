1. Researcher identifies criteria for participants.
2. Recruitment Tool queries potential participants from Database.
3. Database returns list of potential participants to Recruitment Tool.
4. Researcher reviews list of participants.
5. Researcher selects potential interviewees.
6. Recruitment Tool sends invitation for interview to participants.
7. Participants respond to invitation.
8. Recruitment Tool reports responses (accept/decline) to Researcher.
9. Researcher schedules interviews with acceptees.
10. Recruitment Tool confirms interview schedule with participants.
11. Participants confirm attendance.
12. Recruitment Tool finalizes list of interview participants.
