1. Manager accesses Manager Page.
2. Manager clicks on Add new material information.
3. ManagerMaterialController processes addMaterial request.
4. MaterialService adds the new material information.
5. MaterialRepository saves the new material information to the Database.
6. Database executes insert query.
7. MaterialRepository confirms the insertion.
8. MaterialService confirms the insertion.
9. ManagerMaterialController confirms the insertion.
10. ManagerPage displays confirmation of successful addition.
