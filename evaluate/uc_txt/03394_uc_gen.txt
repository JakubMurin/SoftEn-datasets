1. Guest clicks on the registration link in the mail.
2. Page1 receives the token from the mail.
3. API1 checks the validity of the guild registration token.
4. API1 retrieves the existing token from Guest_Token.
5. Guest_Token provides the token to API1.
6. API1 retrieves the pre-registered guild details from Guild.
7. Guild provides the guild name to API1.
8. API1 sends the guild ID, guild name, and email address to Page1.
