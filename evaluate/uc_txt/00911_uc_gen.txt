1. Student takes an exam.
2. List of available automatic exams is displayed.
3. Student selects an exam to take.
4. Exam is built for the student to take.
5. Student answers questions in the exam.
6. Questions are validated.
7. Exam grade on percentage is calculated.
8. Student receives the grade.
9. Student's grade is saved in the system.
10. Feedback is shown to the student.
