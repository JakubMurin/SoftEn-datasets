1. Administrator selects "Generate Nametags".
2. Camp Management System retrieves all Camper Profiles.
3. Camper Profile Database provides Camper Details.
4. Camp Management System sends Camper Details to Nametag Generator.
5. Nametag Generator generates Nametags.
6. Nametags are sent back to Camp Management System.
7. Camp Management System sends Nametags for Printing.
8. Printer acknowledges Printing.
9. Camp Management System confirms and provides Status to Administrator.
