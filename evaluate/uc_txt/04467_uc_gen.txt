1. Apotheker registers for AZ.
2. AVS registers with ADL.
3. ADL provides URLs.
4. AVS generates dataset.
5. AVS signs dataset.
6. AVS uploads signed dataset to UC.
7. UC verifies signature of dataset.
8. UC stores dataset for sync.
9. UC syncs entries with APOVZD.
10. UC deletes entries.
11. APOVZD updates pharmacy with URLs and certificates.
