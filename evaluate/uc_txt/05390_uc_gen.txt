1. Member selects payment option.
2. Front End initiates payment request.
3. Payment Service requests payment processing.
4. Mobile Banking System processes transaction.
5. Mobile Banking System sends transaction authentication to Member.
6. Member authorizes transaction.
7. Mobile Banking System confirms transaction success.
8. Payment Service updates payment status in Database.
9. Database confirms update to Payment Service.
10. Payment Service displays success message in Front End.
11. Front End shows confirmation to Member.
