1. Buyer/Client sends a POST request to create a Fault Management Job.
2. Fault Management Job is created.
3. Fault Management Job enters the 'inProgress' state.
4. Data is generated for the Fault Management Job.
5. Fault Management Report is created due to the Job.
6. Fault Management Report enters the 'inProgress' state.
7. Data is collected for the Fault Management Report.
8. Fault Management Report is completed.
9. Fault Management Job is marked as completed.
10. Buyer/Client requests the Fault Management Report.
11. Fault Management Report content is sent to the Buyer/Client.
