1. Business User registers for Fault Management notifications with the hub.
2. System Operator Facility creates a Fault Management Job.
3. Fault Management Job is transitioned to in-progress state.
4. Fault Management Job generates necessary data.
5. Fault Management Job creates a Fault Management Report.
6. Fault Management Report is transitioned to in-progress state.
7. Fault Management Report collects data.
8. Fault Management Report transitions to completed state.
9. Fault Management Job receives the completed report.
10. Fault Management Job schedules the next job.
11. Fault Management Job transitions to completed or scheduled state.
12. Business User requests to get the generated report.
13. System Operator Facility retrieves and sends back the Fault Management Report.
