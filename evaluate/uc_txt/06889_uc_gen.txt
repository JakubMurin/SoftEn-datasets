1. User configures a house.
2. Request sent to MainUI for configuring a house.
3. Data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) is requested.
4. User inserts data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) into HouseConfigurationUI.
5. House ConfigurationController sets up house.
6. House is reconfigured by the house.
7. House validates the inserted data.
8. Display house info to the User.
