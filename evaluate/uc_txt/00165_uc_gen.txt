1. Administrator Selects "Generate Nametags".
2. CMS Retrieves All Camper Profiles from CPD.
3. CPD Provides Camper Details to CMS.
4. CMS Sends Camper Details to NG.
5. NG Generates Nametags.
6. NG Provides Generated Nametags to CMS.
7. CMS Sends Nametags for Printing to P.
8. P Acknowledges Printing to CMS.
9. CMS Provides Confirmation and Status to Administrator.
