1. RC checks existing templates in the database.
2. If templates are not available, DB informs RC and RC requests specifics from TM.
3. TM provides template specifics to RC.
4. RC creates a draft of the template.
5. RC submits the draft to AA for approval.
6. AA reviews the template draft.
7. If revisions are needed, AA requests revisions from RC.
8. RC revises the template.
9. RC resubmits the revised template to AA for approval.
10. AA approves the template.
11. RC stores the new template in the database.
12. RC informs TM about the availability of the template.
13. If templates are available, DB provides existing templates to RC.
14. RC shares the existing templates with TM.
