1. Customer inserts card.
2. Customer enters PIN.
3. ATM verifies PIN with Bank System.
4. ATM displays PIN verification success.
5. Customer selects Balance Inquiry or Withdrawal.
6. ATM requests balance data from Bank System.
7. ATM displays Balance.
8. ATM requests withdrawal amount from Customer.
9. Customer confirms withdrawal.
10. ATM processes withdrawal.
11. ATM dispenses cash.
12. ATM displays insufficient funds message.
13. Customer selects Cancel.
14. ATM displays transaction canceled message.
15. Customer removes card.
