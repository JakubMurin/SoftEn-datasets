1. End-User requests help from CS.
2. End-User provides NIN to CS.
3. CS passes NIN to Application.
4. CS informs End-User to start Swedish BankID.
5. End-User starts BankID authentication.
6. End-User authenticates with BankID via IDP.
7. IDP provides authentication information to Signicat.
8. Signicat processes the authentication.
9. Application fetches the authentication result from Signicat.
10. CS provides the end-user authentication result.
11. CS assists the authenticated end-user.
