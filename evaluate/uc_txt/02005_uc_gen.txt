1. Buyer/Client sends a POST request to create a Fault Management Job.
2. Fault Management Job is created and set to in progress state.
3. Fault Management Job generates necessary data.
4. Fault Management Report is created causing report creation.
5. Fault Management Report is set to in progress state.
6. Fault Management Report collects required data.
7. Fault Management Report is completed successfully.
8. Fault Management Job is set to completed state.
9. Buyer/Client requests to retrieve the Fault Management Report.
10. Fault Management Report content is sent to the Buyer/Client.
