1. Curator selects item(s) for mapping.
2. UI Interface initiates mapping request with itemIDs, sourceCollection, and targetCollection.
3. Mapping Controller validates itemIDs and collections.
4. Collections Database responds with validation results.
5. Mapping Controller maps item(s) to the target collection.
6. Collections Database confirms the mapping.
7. Mapping Controller displays mapping success message on UI.
8. Curator confirms and closes the mapping process.
