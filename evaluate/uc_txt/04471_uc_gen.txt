1. Apotheker registers for AZ.
2. AVS registers with ADL.
3. AVS generates URLs for delivery.
4. AVS provides URLs.
5. AVS generates dataset.
6. AVS signs dataset with C.HCI.OSIG.
7. AVS uploads signed dataset to UC.
8. UC verifies signature of the dataset.
9. UC stores the dataset for synchronization.
10. UC syncs entries with APOVZD daily from 0-6 am.
11. APOVZD gets certificates from VZD.
12. APOVZD updates pharmacy with URLs and encryption certificates.
