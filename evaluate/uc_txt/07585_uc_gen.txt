1. Researcher accesses the Repository Interface.
2. Repository Interface initiates search for faculty members.
3. SearchSystem queries faculty data in FacultyProfiles.
4. FacultyProfiles returns results to SearchSystem.
5. SearchSystem displays results to the Researcher.
6. Researcher reviews Faculty interests and expertise.
7. Researcher requests detailed information from FacultyProfiles.
8. FacultyProfiles provides detailed information to Researcher.
9. Researcher selects Faculty members for collaboration.
10. Researcher saves or notes selected Faculty for future collaboration in CollaborationModule.
11. CollaborationModule confirms selection and provides next steps to Researcher.
