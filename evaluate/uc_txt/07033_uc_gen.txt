1. The User Researcher identifies criteria for the participants.
2. The Research Recruitment Tool queries the potential participants from the Research Participant Database.
3. The Research Participant Database returns a list of potential participants.
4. The User Researcher reviews the list of potential participants.
5. The User Researcher selects potential interviewees.
6. The Research Recruitment Tool sends invitations for interviews to the selected participants.
7. Participants respond to the invitations.
8. The Research Recruitment Tool reports responses (accept/decline) to the User Researcher.
9. The User Researcher schedules interviews with the participants who accepted the invitation.
10. The Research Recruitment Tool confirms the interview schedules with the participants.
11. The participants confirm their attendance for the interviews.
12. The Research Recruitment Tool finalizes the list of interview participants.
