1. Administrator clicks on the "Surat Lapas" menu.
2. Surat Lapas interface is displayed.
3. Administrator inputs the number and date for the Surat Lapas.
4. System searches for the requested Surat Lapas.
5. If the Surat is available:.
6. BimbinganController searches for additional information about Napi.
7. NapiController retrieves Napi details based on the Surat number and date.
8. Model_Napi retrieves Napi details.
9. System redirects for further steps.
10. If the Surat is not available:.
11. BimbinganController redirects with a message.
12. Administrator receives a message indicating that the Surat is not available.
