1. Curator selects the collection.
2. Curator initiates the deposit.
3. Deposit Interface requests deposit details.
4. Curator provides deposit details (including embargo date).
5. Deposit Interface validates details (including embargo date).
6. Validation Service passes validation.
7. Deposit Interface saves deposit details to the EDRMS Database.
8. Database confirms the save operation.
9. Deposit Interface sends confirmation to Curator.
10. Notification Service notifies Curator of successful deposit.
