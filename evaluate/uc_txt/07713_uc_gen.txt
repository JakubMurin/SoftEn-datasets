1. User clicks on the delete button.
2. PrintscriptUI receives the delete button click event.
3. PrintscriptUI calls SnippetService to delete the snippet.
4. SnippetService checks if the snippet exists.
5. SnippetService deletes the snippet by id.
6. SnippetService deletes related assets.
7. PrintscriptUI displays success message.
8. User sees the success message.
