1. User requests display of attributes.
2. ViewAllClasses receives request and sends getAttributes() to ModelClass.
3. ModelClass returns ArrayList of attributes to ViewAllClasses.
4. ViewAllClasses retrieves attribute details from Attribute.
5. Attribute returns attribute properties to ViewAllClasses.
6. ViewAllClasses processes attributes for display.
7. ViewAllClasses renders attributes in the UI.
8. UI display complete.
