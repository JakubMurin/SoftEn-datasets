1. User requests a secret key generation.
2. SecretKey receives the request.
3. SecretKey invokes SecureRandom.
4. SecureRandom generates random bytes.
5. SecureRandom passes generated bytes to NativeCrypto.
6. NativeCrypto creates a Uint8List from the bytes.
7. SecureRandom receives the Uint8List.
8. SecretKey receives the Uint8List and forms the SecretKey.
