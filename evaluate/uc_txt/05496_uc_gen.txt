5. Secretaria confirms the update with cliente.

9. Cliente requests further consultation with veterinario.
10. Veterinario schedules diagnostic tests for cliente.
11. Veterinario finalizes treatment for cliente.
12. Veterinario registers examination requests for cliente.
13. Veterinario provides historical test results to cliente.
14. Veterinario provides medication prescriptions to cliente.
