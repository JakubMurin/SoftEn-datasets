1. Player starts the game.
2. StartGameMenu checks the user's choice.
3. Player exits the game by pressing the exit button.
4. StartGameMenu initiates the game.
5. GameFrame handles game playing.
6. GameEndMenu appears if the player loses.
7. Player returns to the menu from GameEndMenu.
8. Player begins a new game from GameEndMenu.
9. StartGameMenu deletes game results from the database.
10. ResultsFrame displays the leaderboard for the game.
