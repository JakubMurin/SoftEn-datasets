1. Chef selects a dish to update allergen information.
2. Restaurant Management System requests dish details.
3. Menu Management Subsystem queries dish ingredients.
4. Database returns dish ingredients.
5. Menu Management Subsystem displays ingredients with allergy info.
6. Restaurant Management System shows ingredients and current allergy list to Chef.
7. Chef adds/removes allergens to/from the list.
8. Restaurant Management System updates allergy information.
9. Menu Management Subsystem updates dish ingredient allergy info in Database.
10. Database confirms update.
11. Menu Management Subsystem confirms update to Chef via Restaurant Management System.
12. Restaurant Management System displays update confirmation to Chef.
