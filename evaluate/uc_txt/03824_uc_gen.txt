1. User inputs URL or selects from list.
2. AnalyticsPage receives the input.
3. AnalyticsControl sends data request.
4. URLAnalytics fetches analytics data for the URL.
5. AnalyticsDB queries the analytics data.
6. AnalyticsDB returns the analytics data.
7. URLAnalytics provides the analytics data to AnalyticsControl.
8. AnalyticsControl sends data to AnalyticsPage.
9. AnalyticsPage displays the analytics data for the URL.
