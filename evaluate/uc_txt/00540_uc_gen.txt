1. Student opens the login page.
2. Monitor displays the login page.
3. Student logs in with username and password.
4. Authentication verifies the login credentials.
5. Monitor displays the library page.
6. Student submits book details.
7. Monitor searches for the book.
8. BookManagement retrieves book data.
9. BookManagement lists the available books.
10. Student selects a book to reserve.
11. Monitor receives the book reservation request.
12. Reservation creates a reservation for the book.
13. BookManagement updates the book status.
14. Reservation confirms the reservation.
15. Monitor displays the reservation confirmation.
