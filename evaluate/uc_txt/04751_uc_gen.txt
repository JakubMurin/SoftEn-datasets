1. User requests access to privacy settings and preferences.
2. GUI fetches current privacy settings.
3. Settings retrieves current settings from Storage.
4. Storage returns current settings to the Settings service.
5. Settings displays current privacy settings to GUI.
6. GUI presents privacy setting options to the User.
7. User adjusts privacy and data settings.
8. GUI updates the settings as per the user's selections.
9. Settings saves the new settings in the Storage.
10. Storage confirms that new settings have been saved successfully.
11. Settings informs the GUI that the update was successful.
12. GUI displays a success message to the User.
