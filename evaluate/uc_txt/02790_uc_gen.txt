1. Customer Manager requests to view ordered candidates list.
2. UI presents a list of job openings to the Customer Manager.
3. Customer Manager selects a job opening.
4. Controller lists candidates for the selected job opening.
5. Application Service retrieves candidates related to the job opening.
6. Controller retrieves related job interviews of the candidates.
7. Controller sorts the interviews based on job interview grades.
8. UI displays the ordered list of candidates with their grades to the Customer Manager.
