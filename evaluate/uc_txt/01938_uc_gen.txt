1. Resource_provider compiles the Primary Resource.
2. Compilation_Manager finds specific KieCompilerService for the Primary Resource.
3. KieCompilerService processes the Primary Resource.
4. Compilation_Manager receives the Compiled Class.
5. Resource_provider receives the IndexFile.
6. Resource_provider consumes the IndexFile.
7. Compilation_Manager finds specific KieCompilerService for the Intermediate Resource.
8. KieCompilerService processes the Intermediate Resource.
9. Compilation_Manager receives the Compiled Class.
10. Resource_provider receives the IndexFile.
11. Resource_provider consumes the IndexFile.
