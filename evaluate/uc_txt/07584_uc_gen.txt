1. Researcher accesses the repository interface.
2. Researcher initiates a search for relevant faculty profiles.
3. Research system queries faculty data based on search.
4. Faculty profiles data is returned to researcher.
5. System displays the search results to the researcher.
6. Researcher reviews detailed information about faculty interests/expertise.
7. Researcher requests more detailed information about selected faculty.
8. Faculty profiles provide detailed information to the researcher.
9. Researcher selects faculty members for collaboration.
10. Researcher saves/preferences/notes for collaboration using Collaboration Module.
11. CollaborationModule confirms the collaboration settings and provides next steps to researcher.
