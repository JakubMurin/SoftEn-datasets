1. User posts Driver information (name, csp, driver) to Spider.
2. Spider registers Driver and sends acknowledgment to the User.
3. User posts Region information (name, csp, region, zone) to Spider.
4. Spider registers Region and sends acknowledgment to the User.
5. User posts Credential information (name, csp, credentials) to Spider.
6. Spider stores the Credential information and sends acknowledgment to the User.
7. User posts Connection Configuration (name, csp, driver, credential, region) to Spider.
8. Spider configures the connection and sends acknowledgment to the User.
9. User defines Namespace (name) to Tumblebug.
10. Tumblebug creates the Namespace and notifies the User.
