1. Member selects payment option.
2. Front End initiates payment request.
3. Payment Service requests payment processing.
4. Mobile Banking processes the transaction.
5. Mobile Banking sends transaction authentication to Member.
6. Member authorizes the transaction.
7. Mobile Banking confirms transaction success to Payment Service.
8. Payment Service updates payment status in Database.
9. Database confirms the update to Payment Service.
10. Payment Service displays success message on Front End.
11. Front End shows confirmation to Member.
