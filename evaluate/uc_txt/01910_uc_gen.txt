1. User triggers the onAcceptButton event in the HelloController.
2. HelloController creates a new Bankomat instance.
3. HelloController initializes the Bankomat with selected bank, location, and initial balance.
4. HelloController adds a new UserAdmin to the Bankomat.
5. HelloController sets the Bankomat in the SignIn_UpController.
6. SignIn_UpController displays the Bankomat.
7. HelloController closes the operation.
