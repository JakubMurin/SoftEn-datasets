1. Administrator selects "Generate Nametags".
2. Camp Management System retrieves all camper profiles from Camper Profile Database.
3. Camper Profile Database sends Camper Details to Camp Management System.
4. Camp Management System sends Camper Details to Nametag Generator.
5. Nametag Generator generates Nametags.
6. Nametag Generator sends generated Nametags to Camp Management System.
7. Camp Management System sends Nametags for printing to Printer.
8. Printer acknowledges printing to Camp Management System.
9. Camp Management System sends confirmation and status to Administrator.
