1. User Researcher searches for potential participants based on interview topic.
2. User Research Management System queries the NSF Staff Database for staff with relevant expertise.
3. NSF Staff Database returns a list of potential NSF participants to the User Research Management System.
4. User Research Management System presents potential participants to the User Researcher.
5. User Researcher selects NSF participants for interviews.
6. User Research Management System sends invitation emails with interview details to selected participants.
7. Email System receives the interview invitation.
8. NSF Person RSVPs to the invitation.
9. Email System logs the RSVP status.
10. User Research Management System confirms the RSVPs of selected NSF participants.
