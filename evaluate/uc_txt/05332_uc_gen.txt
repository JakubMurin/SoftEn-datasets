1. User creates a new App instance by invoking .create(App).
2. Liba creates a cInstance.
3. renderC is triggered with the cInstance.
4. App is rendered with properties and renderLiba.
5. App creates a 'button' element.
6. App creates a UserCard element.
7. UserCard is created with props, AppCInstance, and a key.
8. UserCard is rendered with properties and renderLiba.
9. UserCard creates a 'div' element.
10. UserCard creates another 'div' element.
