1. Librarian navigates to BookPage.
2. Librarian clicks on Add Book.
3. BookPage displays Add Book Options Pop-up.
4. Librarian clicks on Add Book Option.
5. BookPage prompts for ISBN input.
6. Librarian enters ISBN.
7. Controller requests Book Information by ISBN.
8. Controller retrieves Book Information by ISBN from OpenLibrary.
9. OpenLibrary sends Book Information to Controller.
10. Controller sends book information to Database.
11. Database adds Book to Database.
12. Database sends response of addition confirmation.
13. Controller receives response of addition confirmation.
14. BookPage displays Addition Confirmation.
