1. Recruitment Coordinator checks for existing templates in the Template Database.
2. If templates are not available, Recruitment Coordinator informs Team Member.
3. Recruitment Coordinator requests specifics for template creation from Team Member.
4. Team Member provides template specifics.
5. Recruitment Coordinator creates a draft template.
6. Recruitment Coordinator submits the draft template for approval to Approval Authority.
7. Approval Authority reviews the template draft.
8. Approval Authority requests revisions from Recruitment Coordinator if needed.
9. Recruitment Coordinator revises the template as per request.
10. Recruitment Coordinator resubmits the revised template for approval to Approval Authority.
11. Approval Authority approves the template.
12. Recruitment Coordinator stores the new template in the Template Database.
13. Recruitment Coordinator informs Team Member about the availability of the approved template.
