1. User moves the map.
2. Frontend calculates the area.
3. Frontend adds the area to the Split Queue.
4. Frontend retrieves the depth in the area.
5. Backend requests Points in the depth.
6. Points in Area are received.
7. Split Map requests points not calculated.
8. Calculate retrieves undefined points.
9. Calculate calculates points.
10. Update Points.
11. Split Map inserts with undefined depth.
12. Backend creates overlay with current points.
13. Frontend shows overlay to User.
