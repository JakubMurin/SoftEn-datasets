1. Archivist signs in to the system.
2. Archivist accesses the upload interface.
3. Archivist chooses inventory file(s).
4. Archivist provides metadata.
5. Archivist defines access rights.
6. Metadata and access rights are saved in the database.
7. Archivist uploads file(s) to the system.
8. Files are successfully stored in the archive.
9. Inventory list is updated in the database.
10. New entries are added to the archival collections.
11. Archivist is notified of successful upload.
12. Notification is sent to donor representatives.
