1. User presses the volume button on the A2DP Sink.
2. AVRCP volume change detected by A2DP Sink.
3. AVRCP sends volume change message to Service Bluetooth.
4. Service Bluetooth shares volume change message with Service Audio.
5. Service Audio adjusts volume to the received level.
6. Service Audio notifies Application about the updated volume.
7. Application displays the current volume level to User.
