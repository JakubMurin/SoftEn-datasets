1. Team Member accesses the feature backlog and lists proposed features.
2. Team Member reviews the listed features.
3. Team Member consults the prioritization criteria and understands the guidelines.
4. Team Member applies the criteria to the features.
5. Team Member consults with Designer regarding the filtered features.
6. Designer provides design feasibility.
7. Team Member consults with Engineer on technical feasibility.
8. Engineer provides technical assessment.
9. Team Member updates the prioritized features list in the CMS/Backlog.
10. Team Member shares the prioritized features list with the Engineer and Designer.
11. Engineer updates technical details in the CMS.
12. Evaluation decisions received concerning the feature feasibility and design.
13. Finalize feature prioritization based on confirmation received.
