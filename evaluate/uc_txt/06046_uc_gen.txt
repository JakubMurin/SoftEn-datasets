1. Curator selects the collection and initiates the deposit.
2. Curator requests deposit details from Deposit Interface.
3. Curator provides deposit details including embargo date to Deposit Interface.
4. Deposit Interface validates details including embargo date.
5. Deposit Interface saves deposit details into EDRMS Database.
6. EDRMS Database confirms the save operation.
7. Notification Service sends confirmation of successful deposit to Curator.
