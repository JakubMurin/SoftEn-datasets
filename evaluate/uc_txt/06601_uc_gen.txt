1. The Counselor logs in to the camp platform.
2. The counselor fetches the list of assigned campers from the camp database.
3. The camp platform displays the list of assigned campers to the counselor.
4. The counselor selects "Take Attendance" from the options.
5. For each camper, the counselor marks the attendance by providing the Camper ID and their status.
6. The camp platform updates the attendance records in the database.
7. The camp platform displays a confirmation of the attendance update to the counselor.
8. The camp platform saves the attendance records in the database.
9. The camp platform displays the final confirmation of the attendance records being saved.
