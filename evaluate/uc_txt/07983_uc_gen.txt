1. View POA Requests List on the vets-website through GET request to the vets-api.
2. Retrieve and display the POA requests list from the VA.gov Database on the Frontend.
3. Select a specific POA request to view its details.
4. Retrieve and display the detailed information of the selected POA request on the Frontend.
5. Submit a decision (Accept/Decline) for the selected POA request.
6. Process the decision submission and update the status accordingly.
7. Display the success or error response message to the Accredited Representative.
