1. The student chooses an exam to take.
2. The UI sends the chosen exam to the controller.
3. The controller retrieves the exam from the repository.
4. The controller obtains grammar tools from the context.
5. The controller generates the exam using an external service.
6. The controller creates an ExamToBeTakenDTO.
7. The controller sends the ExamToBeTakenDTO to the UI.
8. The UI presents the exam to the student.
