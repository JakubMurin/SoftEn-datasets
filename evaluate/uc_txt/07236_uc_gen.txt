1. Administrator selects "Generate Nametags".
2. CMS retrieves all Camper Profiles.
3. CPD provides Camper Details.
4. CMS sends Camper Details to NG.
5. NG generates Nametags.
6. NG sends Nametags to CMS.
7. CMS sends Nametags for Printing to P.
8. P acknowledges Printing to CMS.
9. CMS confirms and provides Status to Administrator.
