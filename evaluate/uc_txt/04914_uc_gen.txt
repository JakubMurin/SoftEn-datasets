1. User Researcher searches for potential participants based on interview topic.
2. User Research Management System queries NSF Staff Database for staff with relevant expertise.
3. NSF Staff Database returns list of potential NSF participants.
4. User Research Management System presents potential participants to User Researcher.
5. User Researcher selects NSF participants for interviews.
6. User Research Management System sends invitation emails with interview details.
7. NSF Persons receive interview invitations.
8. NSF Persons RSVP to the invitation.
9. Email System logs RSVP status.
10. User Research Management System confirms NSF participant RSVPs.
