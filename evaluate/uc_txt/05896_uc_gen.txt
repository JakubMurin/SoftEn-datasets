1. Researcher defines criteria for participant selection.
2. RecruitmentPlatform queries potential participants based on criteria from UserDb.
3. UserDb returns a list of potential participants to RecruitmentPlatform.
4. RecruitmentPlatform sends recruitment invitations to Participants.
5. Participant responds to the invitation.
6. RecruitmentPlatform provides a list of participating users to Researcher.
7. Researcher conducts user research sessions with Participants.
8. Participant provides feedback and data to Researcher.
9. Researcher inputs user research data into AnalysisSystem.
10. AnalysisSystem analyzes and summarizes findings.
11. AnalysisSystem provides synthesized results to Researcher.
12. Researcher updates project status to "Complete" on RecruitmentPlatform.
13. Researcher archives project data and findings on AnalysisSystem.
