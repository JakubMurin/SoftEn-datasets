1. Ayeesha requests to register their chequing account from DFSP A using the PISP App.
2. DFSP generates a random UUID and saves it internally.
3. DFSP provides the generated UUID to PISP for account linking.
4. PISP associates Ayeesha's chequing account with the provided UUID.
5. DFSP sends a request to the Switch to create an association.
6. Switch confirms the creation of the association and informs ALS.
7. ALS acknowledges the creation of the association.
8. Switch notifies DFSP about the successful creation of the association.
9. DFSP confirms the association creation to PISP.
