1. Admin selects A/V Quality Filter Option.
2. Admin Interface initiates A/V Quality Filtering.
3. Video Management System sends video for A/V analysis.
4. A/V Quality Assessment Module analyzes video A/V quality.
5. A/V Quality Assessment Module stores A/V quality scores in Database.
6. A/V Analysis Complete.
7. Ranking Algorithm sends scores for ranking.
8. Ranking Algorithm calculates rankings based on A/V Quality.
9. Ranking Algorithm updates rankings in Database.
10. Database sends filtered and ranked video list to Video Management System.
11. Video Management System displays ranked videos to Admin.
12. Admin Interface shows filtered and ranked videos to Admin.
