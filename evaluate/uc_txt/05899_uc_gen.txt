1. Researcher defines criteria for participant selection.
2. RecruitmentPlatform queries potential participants based on criteria.
3. UserDb returns list of potential participants.
4. RecruitmentPlatform sends recruitment invitations.
5. Participant responds to invitation.
6. RecruitmentPlatform provides list of participating users.
7. Researcher conducts user research sessions with Participant.
8. Participant provides feedback and data to Researcher.
9. Researcher inputs user research data to AnalysisSystem.
10. AnalysisSystem analyzes and summarizes findings.
11. AnalysisSystem provides synthesized results to Researcher.
12. Researcher updates project status to "Complete" in RecruitmentPlatform.
13. Researcher archives project data and findings in AnalysisSystem.
