1. User requests to register.
2. Frontend shows registration form to the User.
3. User fills in the registration form.
4. Frontend shows consent form to the User.
5. User accepts the consent.
6. Frontend validates the form.
7. Frontend forwards the registration data to Middleware.
8. Middleware posts the registration data.
9. Middleware resolves registration (success).
10. Frontend shows success message to the User.
