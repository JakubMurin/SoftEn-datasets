1. The operator accesses the digital form.
2. The digital form interface displays the form to the operator.
3. The operator enters the subject responses into the form.
4. The form interface posts the responses to the responsesAPI.
5. The responsesAPI saves the responses in the Neurohub Database.
6. The responsesAPI confirms the successful save of responses in the database.
7. The responsesAPI confirms the success to the form interface.
8. The form interface displays a success message to the operator.
