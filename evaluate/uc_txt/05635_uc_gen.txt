1. User accesses platform.
2. Authentication system successfully authenticates the user.
3. User navigates to My Schedule on the Dashboard.
4. Dashboard displays registered sessions to the User.
5. User selects Edit on a session.
6. Session Management displays session details form to the User.
7. User submits updated session details.
8. Session Management validates and updates session details in the Database.
9. Database confirms the update.
10. Session Management displays success message to the User.
