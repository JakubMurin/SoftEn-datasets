1. Caregiver requests to enable Step Counter feature for User.
2. ALFRED Device activates Step Counter.
3. ALFRED Software confirms Step Counter activation.
4. ALFRED Device notifies User about Step Counter availability.
5. User enables Step Counter.
6. ALFRED Device initiates Step Counting.
7. User walks, ALFRED Device counts steps.
8. ALFRED Software sends daily step data to Health Monitoring Service.
9. Health Monitoring Service acknowledges receipt of daily step data.
10. Caregiver requests Step Count Data from ALFRED Device.
11. ALFRED Device retrieves Step Data from ALFRED Software.
12. ALFRED Software requests User's Step Data from Health Monitoring Service.
13. Health Monitoring Service sends User's Step Data to ALFRED Software.
14. ALFRED Software displays Step Data.
15. ALFRED Device shows Step Data to Caregiver.
