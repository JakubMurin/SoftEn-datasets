2. ARS queries audit data from DB.
3. ARS generates the audit report.
4. ARS logs the audit report request.
5. ARS sends the audit report to Repoadmin.
6. Repoadmin requests error logs.
7. ARS queries error logs from DB.

11. Repoadmin subscribes to reports.
12. ARS logs the subscription.
