1. Camp Administrator requests to view medical forms.
2. Camp Management Platform queries medical forms from Database.
3. Database returns medical forms to Camp Management Platform.
4. Camp Management Platform displays medical forms to Camp Administrator.
5. Camp Administrator selects a specific camper.
6. Camp Management Platform queries the camper's medical form from Database.
7. Database returns the selected camper's medical form to Camp Management Platform.
8. Camp Management Platform displays the selected camper's medical form to Camp Administrator.
