1. User configures a house.
2. MainUI forwards configuration process to HouseConfigurationUI.
3. HouseConfigurationUI requests data including latitude, longitude, zipcode, and address from User.
4. User inserts data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) into HouseConfigurationUI.
5. HouseConfigurationUI sets the house.
6. HouseConfigurationController reconfigures the house based on data received.
7. House validates the data.
8. House displays the configured house data to User.
