1. Student takes an exam.
2. List of available exams is displayed.
3. Student selects an exam to take.
4. Validation is performed to check if the exam is open for submission.
5. If exam is open for submission, the exam is prepared for the student.
6. Student answers questions in the exam.
7. Question validation is performed.
8. Exam grade is calculated based on the percentage of correct answers.
9. Grade is saved.
10. Feedback is provided to the student.
