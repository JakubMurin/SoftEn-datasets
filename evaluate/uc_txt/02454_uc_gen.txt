1. Buyer/Client requests creation of a new Fault Management Job.
2. Fault Management Job is created.
3. Fault Management Job is set to in-progress state.
4. Data for the job is generated.
5. Reports production process is initiated.
6. Fault Management Report is created and set to in-progress state.
7. Data for the report is collected.
8. Report finalization process is initiated.
9. Fault Management Report is completed.

11. Buyer/Client retrieves the Fault Management Report.
