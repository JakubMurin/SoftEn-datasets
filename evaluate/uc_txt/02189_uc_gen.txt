1. Archivist requests to restrict file.
2. Archivist credentials are validated by the Web Interface.
3. The relevant form for further restriction details is presented by the Web Interface.
4. The Archivist submits restriction details.
5. The file metadata is updated with the restriction details by the Database.
6. The access restrictions applied successfully by the File Management System.
7. A success message with the outcome is displayed to the Archivist.
