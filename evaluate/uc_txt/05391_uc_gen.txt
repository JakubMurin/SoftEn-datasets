1. Member selects payment option.
2. Front End initiates payment request.
3. Payment Service requests payment processing from Mobile Banking System.
4. Mobile Banking System processes the transaction.
5. Mobile Banking System sends transaction authentication to Member.
6. Member authorizes the transaction on Mobile Banking System.
7. Payment Service confirms successful transaction, updates payment status in Database, and displays success message on Front End.
8. Front End shows confirmation to Member.
