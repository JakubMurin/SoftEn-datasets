1. Repository Manager accesses Replication Policy Settings through EDRMS Interface.
2. Retrieves Current Policy Settings through Replication Policy Module.
3. Queries Existing Policies from EDRMS Database.
4. Returns Policies to Replication Policy Module.
5. Shows Current Policies to EDRMS Interface.
6. Displays Policies to Repository Manager.
7. Defines/Updates Policy for Collection through EDRMS Interface.
8. Processes Policy Specifications with Policy Patch.
9. Updates Policy in Database.
10. Confirms the Update of Policy in DB.
11. Initiates Policy Replication Update with Remote Storage Service.
12. Acknowledges Replication Policy Update to Policy Patch.
13. Confirms Policy Update to EDRMS Interface.
14. Shows Update Confirmation to Repository Manager.
