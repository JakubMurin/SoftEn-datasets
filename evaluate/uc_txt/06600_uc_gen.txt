1. Counselor logs into the Camp Platform.
2. Counselor fetches the list of assigned campers.
3. Camp Platform displays the list of assigned campers to the Counselor.
4. Counselor selects "Take Attendance" for a camper.
5. For each camper, Counselor marks attendance.
6. Camp Platform updates the attendance status in the database.
7. Camp Platform displays confirmation of the attendance update to the Counselor.
8. Camp Platform saves the attendance records in the database.
9. Camp Platform displays final confirmation of attendance recording to the Counselor.
