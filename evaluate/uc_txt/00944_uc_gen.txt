1. Curator selects item(s) for mapping.
2. UI initiates mapping request(itemIDs, sourceCollection, targetCollection).
3. Mapping Controller validates the itemIDs and collections.
4. Collections Database responds with validation result.
5. Mapping Controller maps item(s) to target collection.
6. Collections Database confirms mapping.
7. Mapping Controller displays mapping success message.
8. Curator confirms and closes.
