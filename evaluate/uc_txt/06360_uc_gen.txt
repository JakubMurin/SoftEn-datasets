1. Client makes a POST request for a Fault Management Job.
2. Fault Management Job is created.
3. Job is set to 'inProgress' state.
4. Data is generated for the Job.
5. Report is created for the Fault Management Job.
6. Report is set to 'inProgress' state.
7. Report data is collected.
8. Report is marked as completed.
9. Fault Management Job is marked as completed.
10. Client requests the Fault Management Report.
11. Fault Management Report content is delivered to the Client.
