1. Researcher accesses the repository.
2. UI initiates a search for faculty profiles.
3. Search queries faculty data in Profiles.
4. Profiles return results to Search.
5. Search displays results to UI.
6. Researcher reviews faculty interests and expertise.

8. Profiles provide detailed information to UI.

10. UI saves/notes selected faculty for collaboration.
11. Collab confirms the selection and provides next steps to Researcher.
