1. Repoadmin accesses the SLA management section in the EDRMS Portal.
2. The EDRMS Portal retrieves SLA details from the SLA Database.
3. The SLA Database provides the SLA Data to the EDRMS Portal.
4. The EDRMS Portal displays the SLA details to Repoadmin.
5. Repoadmin selects the SLA details to share.
6. Repoadmin provides the SLA details to the Data Contributor.
7. The Data Contributor acknowledges the receipt of the SLA details to Repoadmin.
