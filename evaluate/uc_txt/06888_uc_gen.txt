1. User configures a house.
2. MainUI requests to configure a house.
3. HouseConfigurationUI requests data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) from User.
4. User inserts data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) in HouseConfigurationUI.
5. HouseConfigurationUI sets house in HouseConfigurationController.
6. HouseConfigurationController reconfigures the house.
7. House validates the data.
8. House displays the configured house to User.
