1. MRI Operator accesses the digital form.
2. Digital Form Interface displays the form to the MRI Operator.
3. MRI Operator enters subject responses into the form.
4. Digital Form Interface posts the responses to the Subject Responses API.
5. Subject Responses API saves the responses to the Neurohub Database.
6. Neurohub Database confirms the successful saving of responses.
7. Subject Responses API confirms the successful saving to the Digital Form Interface.
8. Digital Form Interface displays a success message to the MRI Operator.
