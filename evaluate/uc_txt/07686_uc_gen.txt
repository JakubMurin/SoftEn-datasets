1. User initiates the campaign change process.
2. UMP receives the request to change marketing activity.
3. Retail-UMP identifies and processes the type of change needed (add, invalidate, edit, delete).
4. Retail-UMP synchronously/asynchronously sends a message to Customer.
5. Return to Retail-UMP after sending.
6. Retail-UMP communicates the status update back to UMP.
7. UMP informs the user about the changes.
