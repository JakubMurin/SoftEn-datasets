1. The delivery UAVs are activated and armed.
2. The Drone Commander selects target GPS coordinates from a map.
3. The UAV requests flight authorization.
4. The DroneResponse commander issues a command to start the delivery mission.
5. The UAV performs synchronized takeoff.
6. The UAV flies to the target destination.
7. The UAV drops the item.
8. The UAV establishes a safe flying altitude and sets its target destination to its home coordinates, then flies to the target destination.
9. Once the UAV arrives at its home coordinates, it lands.
