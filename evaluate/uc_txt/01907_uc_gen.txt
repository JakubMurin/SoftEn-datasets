1. User onAcceptButton(ActionEvent) triggers login process.
2. HelloController creates a Bankomat instance.
3. HelloController initializes a new Bankomat with specific details.
4. HelloController adds a new UserAdmin to the Bankomat.
5. HelloController sets the Bankomat in SignIn_UpController.
6. SignIn_UpController displays the Bankomat.
7. HelloController closes the process.
