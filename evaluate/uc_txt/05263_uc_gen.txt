1. Librarian navigates to BookPage.
2. Librarian clicks on Add Book.
3. BookPage displays Add Book Options Pop-up.
4. Librarian clicks on Add Book Option.
5. BookPage displays ISBN Input Popup.
6. Librarian enters ISBN.
7. Controller requests Book Information by ISBN.
8. Controller receives Book Information.
9. BookPage navigates to AddBookPage.
10. Librarian fills in Book Information Form.
11. Controller sends Book Information.
12. Database adds Book to Database.
13. Controller receives Addition Confirmation.
14. BookPage displays Addition Confirmation.
