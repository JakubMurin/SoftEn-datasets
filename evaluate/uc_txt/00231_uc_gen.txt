1. Wang requests to purchase an iPhone 2017 from js.
2. js quotes the price of 10000 yuan for the iPhone 2017 to Wang.
3. If Wang agrees to the price,
            Wang makes a payment of 10000 yuan via alipay.
4. alipay processes the payment and transfers 10000 yuan to js.
5. js confirms the transaction and gives the iPhone 2017 to Wang.
6. If Wang does not agree to the price,
            Wang requests a loan of 10000 yuan from debit.
7. debit provides a loan of 10000 yuan to Wang at a monthly interest rate of 10%.
8. Wang provides the 10000 yuan to js.
9. js gives the iPhone 2017 to Wang.
10. Wang undergoes financial constraints and struggles financially for six months.
