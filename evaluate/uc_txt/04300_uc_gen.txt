1. Navigate to 'Submit Session'.
2. Display 'Session Submission Form'.
3. Fill in and Submit Form.
4. Save Session Details in Database.
5. Trigger Session Review Process.
6. Update Session Status to 'Under Review'.
7. Trigger Notification to User: 'Session Submitted'.
8. Refresh/Check Email.
9. Update Session Status to Approved/Rejected.
10. Trigger Notification to User: 'Session Evaluated'.
11. Receive Email Notification: 'Session Approved' or 'Session Rejected'.
