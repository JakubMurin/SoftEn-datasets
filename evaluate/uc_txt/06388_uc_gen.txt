1. User inputs form parameters.
2. View Controller activates to process the form.

4. Service activates and launches statistic computation.
5. Service contacts TbCountryTourMapper function to retrieve data.
6. Mapper conducts a successful query.
7. Service receives the query result.
8. Service returns data to the View Controller.
9. Data transferred to the frontend.
