1. Admin initiates the creation of a new user.
2. EletricGo is activated.
3. EletricGo requests data (first name, last name, email, password, password confirmation, phone number, user type) from Admin.
4. EletricGo deactivates.
5. Admin enters data (first name, last name, email, password, password confirmation, phone number, user type) into EletricGo.
6. EletricGo is activated.
7. EletricGo deactivates.
8. EletricGo shows data and requests confirmation from Admin.
9. Admin confirms.
10. EletricGo is activated.
11. EletricGo informs the success of the operation to Admin.
12. EletricGo deactivates.
