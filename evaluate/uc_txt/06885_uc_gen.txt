1. User requests to configure a house.
2. MainUI prompts the user to configure a house.
3. User navigates to the HouseConfigurationUI to configure a house.
4. HouseConfigurationUI requests data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) from the User.
5. User inserts the data (latitude, longitude, zipcode, address) into the HouseConfigurationUI.
6. HouseConfigurationUI sends the data to HouseConfigurationController to set up the house.
7. HouseConfigurationController configures the house based on the received data.
8. House validates the data.
9. House displays the configured house to the User.
