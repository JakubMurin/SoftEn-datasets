1. Acquirer makes an OFFER to Provider.
2. Provider receives the OFFER.
3. Acquirer sends a REQUEST to Provider.
4. Provider makes a counter OFFER or abandons negotiation.
5. Acquirer sends another REQUEST to Provider.
6. Acquirer receives a REQUEST from Provider.
7. Provider agrees on the negotiated matter.
8. Provider sends an ACKnowledgement to Acquirer.
