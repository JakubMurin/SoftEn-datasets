1. User writes username in TextField.
2. PacManController updates GUI.
3. PacManController gets text from TextField.
4. TextField sends username to PacManController.
5. PacManController validates username with PacManUser.
6. PacManUser confirms username validation.
7. PacManController enables startButton.
8. User clicks startButton.
9. startButton click handled by PacManController.
10. PacManController sets username.
11. PacManController sets score.
12. Game starts.
13. Game over.
14. PacManController saves highscore.
15. PacManPersistence reads scores from file.
16. PacManPersistence sends scores to PacManUser.
17. PacManUser receives scores and sends to PacManPersistence.
18. PacManPersistence writes new score to file.
