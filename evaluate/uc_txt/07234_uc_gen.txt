1. Caregiver requests ALFRED Device to enable Step Counter feature for User.
2. ALFRED Device activates Step Counter.
3. ALFRED Device informs User that Step Counter feature is available.
4. User enables Step Counter.
5. ALFRED Device initiates Step Counting.
6. User walks, ALFRED Device counts steps.
7. ALFRED Device sends daily step data to Health Monitoring Service.
8. Health Monitoring Service acknowledges receipt of daily step data.
9. Caregiver requests Step Count Data from ALFRED Device.
10. ALFRED Device retrieves Step Data from ALFRED Software.
11. ALFRED Software requests User's Step Data from Health Monitoring Service.
12. Health Monitoring Service sends Step Data to ALFRED Software.
13. ALFRED Software displays Step Data.
14. ALFRED Device shows Step Data to Caregiver.
