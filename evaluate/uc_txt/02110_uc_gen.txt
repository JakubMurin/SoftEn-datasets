1. Atendente initiates the loan for a reader.
2. System starts by identifying the reader.
3. Bank: Reader confirms their identity.
4. System creates the loan object.
5. Bank: Emprestimo/Devolucao receives and confirms object creation.
6. Creator associates the loan object with the reader selected.
7. Bank: Emprestimo/Devolucao finishes association confirmation.
8. System informs clerk on loan start.
