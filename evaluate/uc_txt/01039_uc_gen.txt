1. Client generates a client keypair using the Facilitator SDK.
2. Client sends job details and public key to the Facilitator.
3. Facilitator selects Validator and Miner.
4. Facilitator sends job details to the Validator.
5. Validator sends initial job details to the Miner.
6. Miner checks for a free executor, possibly spins up a new one.
7.  Continue with additional steps as required .
