1. SOR registers service and reports current node's network status (bandwidth, latency), node configuration (CPU, memory) information to 目录服务器.
2. 目录服务器 identifies uploaded device information and stores it.
3. 目录服务器 recognizes point-to-point communication relationships and current node's network status.
4. 目录服务器 returns forwarding tasks to be executed by SOR.
5. SOR executes forwarding tasks.
