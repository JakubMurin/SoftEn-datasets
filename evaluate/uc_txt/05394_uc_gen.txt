1. Member selects payment option.
2. Front End initiates payment request to Payment Service.
3. Payment Service requests payment processing from Mobile Banking System.
4. Mobile Banking System processes the transaction and sends authentication to the Member.
5. Member authorizes the transaction.
6. Mobile Banking System confirms transaction success to Payment Service.
7. Payment Service updates payment status in the Database.
8. Database confirms the update to Payment Service.
9. Payment Service displays success message through Front End to the Member.
10. Front End shows confirmation to the Member.
