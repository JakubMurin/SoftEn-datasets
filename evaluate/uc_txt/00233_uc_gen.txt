1. Wang asks the vendor for an iPhone 2017.
2. Vendor offers the iPhone at the price of 10000 Yuan.
3. Wang either makes a payment through Alipay or borrows money if he cannot afford it.
4. If Wang has money, he pays via Alipay, and the transaction is successful.
5. If Wang does not have money, he borrows 10000 Yuan from a shady loan company.
6. The loan company charges 10% monthly interest.
7. Wang returns the money to the vendor.
8. In case of borrowing, Wang suffers financial troubles and struggles for six months.
