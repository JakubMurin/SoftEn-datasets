1. Buyer/Client sends a POST request to create a Fault Management Job.
2. Fault Management Job is created.
3. Fault Management Job enters inProgress state.
4. Fault Management Job generates data.
5. Fault Management Report is created as a result of Job.
6. Fault Management Report enters inProgress state.
7. Fault Management Report collects data.
8. Fault Management Report completes data collection.
9. Fault Management Job is marked as completed.
10. Buyer/Client requests the Fault Management Report content.
11. Fault Management Report content is provided to the Buyer/Client.
