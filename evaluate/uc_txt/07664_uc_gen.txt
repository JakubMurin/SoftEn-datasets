1. Sponsor enters credentials.
2. Authentication System validates credentials.
3. Sponsor requests available summits.
4. Summit Coordination System fetches summits matching brand criteria.
5. Summit Coordination System provides summit details.
6. Sponsorship Management System lists available summits.
7. Sponsor selects a summit to sponsor.
8. Sponsorship Management System displays sponsorship levels and benefits.
9. Sponsor chooses a sponsorship level.
10. Sponsorship Management System provides sponsorship details and payment information form.
11. Sponsor submits payment information.
12. Payment System processes payment.
13. Payment System confirms payment.
14. Payment System notifies Sponsorship Management System of payment success.
15. Summit Coordination System assigns Sponsor to Summit.
16. Summit Coordination System confirms sponsorship.
17. Sponsorship Management System provides sponsorship confirmation and receipt.
