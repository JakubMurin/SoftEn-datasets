1. Administrator selects "Generate Nametags".
2. Camp Management System retrieves all camper profiles from the database.
3. Nametag Generator receives the camper details from the Camper Profile Database.
4. Nametag Generator generates nametags for campers.
5. Nametags are sent for printing.
6. Printer acknowledges the printing task.
7. Confirmation and status are communicated back to the Administrator.
