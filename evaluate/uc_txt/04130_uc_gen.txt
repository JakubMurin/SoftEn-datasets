1. The Chef accesses the inventory management system.
2. The system checks the current backup food stock in the InventoryDB.
3. The InventoryDB returns the current stock levels to the system.
4. The system shows the current backup food list with stock levels to the Chef.
5. The Chef identifies items for renewal.
6. The system updates the backup food list in the InventoryDB.
7. The InventoryDB confirms the updated list to the system.
8. The system displays the confirmation of the renewed list to the Chef.
