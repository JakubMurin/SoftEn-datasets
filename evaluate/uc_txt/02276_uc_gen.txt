1. OtherWeb initiates login process with AuthApp.
2. AuthApp fetches app info from AuthServer.
3. AuthServer provides app info to AuthApp.
4. AuthApp connects to Nami wallet.
5. AuthApp fetches random number from AuthServer.
6. AuthServer provides random number and sets access token.
7. AuthApp requests Nami signature.
8. Nami provides signature to AuthApp.
9. AuthApp logs in with AuthServer.
10. AuthServer responds with login success.
11. AuthApp redirects user back to OtherWeb.
