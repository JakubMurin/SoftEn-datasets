1. Alice creates grants treasury.
2. Alice gets treasury address.
3. Alice creates forum proposal.
4. Alice lists proposals from the forum.
5. Alice puts positive/negative feedback on forum.
6. Alice delegates votes in the community.
7. Alice submits treasury role proposal to BitDAO governance.
8. Token holders vote on the treasury role proposal.
9. BitDAO admin gets the result of Alice's proposal.
10. BitDAO admin executes the proposal.
11. Funds are transferred from BitDAO treasury to grants treasury.
12. Confirmation of funds transfer to grants treasury.
13. Alice executes the proposal.
