1. User initiates acquiring a place through labgrid client.
2. Labgrid client sends a request to acquire the place in labgrid coordinator.
3. Labgrid coordinator verifies the acquisition request with the place.
4. Place confirms the acquisition request with labgrid coordinator.
5. Labgrid coordinator informs labgrid client about successful place acquisition.
6. Labgrid client notifies the user that the place has been successfully acquired.
7. User accesses the place through labgrid client console.
8. Labgrid client establishes a connection with the boss publisher.
9. Boss publisher opens a session and presents configuration details.
