1. Firefighters request flight authorization.
2. UAVs are activated and armed.
3. The UAVs perform synchronized takeoff.
4. The UAVs use their onboard vision to create an initial mapping of the building.
5. The UAVs plan optimized and coordinated surveillance flights around the building.
6. Prior to each leg of the surveillance flight, each UAV leases the necessary airspace.
7. Each UAV performs image capture and analysis using thermal imagery.
8. Imagery from each UAV is continuously streamed and aggregated to create a current heatmap of the building.
9. Steps 4-7 are repeated throughout the fire event.
10. The Incident Commander ends the mission once the fire has been controlled.
