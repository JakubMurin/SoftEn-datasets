1. The Manager requests the inventory list through the Management Interface.
2. The Management Interface activates and retrieves the inventory data from the Inventory Management Module.
3. The Inventory Management Module queries the inventory list from the Database.
4. The Database provides the inventory list data to the Inventory Management Module.
5. The Inventory Management Module returns the inventory list to the Management Interface.
6. The Management Interface displays the inventory list to the Manager.
