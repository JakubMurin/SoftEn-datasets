1. Recruitment Coordinator checks existing templates in the Template Database.
2. If templates are not available, Recruitment Coordinator requests specifics from Team Member.
3. Recruitment Coordinator creates a template draft.
4. Recruitment Coordinator submits the draft for approval to Approval Authority.
5. Approval Authority reviews the template draft.
6. If revisions are needed, Approval Authority requests revisions from Recruitment Coordinator.
7. Recruitment Coordinator revises the template.
8. Recruitment Coordinator resubmits the revised template for approval.
9. Approval Authority approves the template.
10. Recruitment Coordinator stores the new template in the database.
11. Recruitment Coordinator informs Team Member about the availability of the new template.
12. If templates are available, Database provides existing templates to Recruitment Coordinator.
13. Recruitment Coordinator shares the existing templates with Team Member.
