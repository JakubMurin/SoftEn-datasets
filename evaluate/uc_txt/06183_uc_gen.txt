1. Admin requests to view list of blog posts.
2. List of blog posts view sends request to BlogController.
3. BlogController checks data.
4. BlogController retrieves list of blog posts from BlogModel.
5. BlogModel returns the list of blog posts to BlogController.
6. BlogController sends the list of blog posts results to list of blog posts view.
7. List of blog posts view displays the list of blog posts to Admin.
