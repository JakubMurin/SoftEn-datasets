1. Apotheker registers for AZ.
2. AVS registers with Apothekendienstleister.
3. AVS provides URLs.
4. AVS generates dataset.
5. AVS signs dataset.
6. AVS uploads signed dataset to APOVZD Upload-Container.
7. APOVZD Upload-Container verifies signature.
8. APOVZD Upload-Container stores dataset for sync.
9. UC syncs entries with APOVZD.
10. UC deletes entries.
11. APOVZD gets certificates from VZD.
12. APOVZD updates pharmacy URLs and certificates.
