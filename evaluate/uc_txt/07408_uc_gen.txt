1. User clicks on the Accept Button triggering an ActionEvent.
2. HelloController creates a new Bankomat instance.
3. Bankomat is initialized with the selected bank, location, and starting balance of 100000.0 implemented by HelloController.
4. HelloController adds a new UserAdmin named "admin" linked to the bankomat with the PIN 5555.
5. HelloController passes the bankomat instance to SignIn_UpController for interaction.
6. SignIn_UpController is shown to the user, interacting with bankomat object.
7. HelloController completes this process by closing the interaction.
