1. Depositor accesses the deposit interface.
2. WebUI requests authentication.
3. Pure authenticates the Depositor.
4. WebUI shows dataset deposit form to Depositor.
5. Depositor fills in form (includes metadata and files).
6. WebUI processes deposit form.
7. DMS checks dataset metadata alignment with Pure requirements.
8. DMS deposits dataset and metadata to Archive.
9. Archive confirms deposit and generates DOI.
10. DMS links dataset DOI with Pure records and updates them.
11. DMS confirms dataset deposit and provides DOI to depositor.
12. WebUI displays confirmation including DOI link.
