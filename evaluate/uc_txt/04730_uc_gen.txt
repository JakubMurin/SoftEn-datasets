1. Administrator selects a group to modify.
2. Camp Management System retrieves the selected group information from the database.
3. Database returns the group information to Camp Management System.
4. Camp Management System displays the group information to the administrator.
5. Administrator modifies the group information.
6. Camp Management System updates the group information in the database.
7. Database confirms the update to Camp Management System.
8. Camp Management System displays the update confirmation to the administrator.
