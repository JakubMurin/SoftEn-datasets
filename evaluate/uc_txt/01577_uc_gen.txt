1. User sends a GET request to CatalogDao.
2. CatalogDao forwards the request to RemoveBookFromCatalogActivity with RemoveBookFromCatalogRequest.
3. RemoveBookFromCatalogActivity executes the removal process.
4. RemoveBookFromCatalogActivity sends a response to CatalogDao.
5. CatalogDao completes the removal process.
6. CatalogDao sends a response to User.
