1. Researcher accesses the Repository Interface.
2. Researcher initiates a search for faculty members.
3. SearchSystem queries faculty data.
4. FacultyProfiles return search results.
5. SearchSystem displays search results.
6. Researcher reviews faculty interests and expertise.
7. Researcher requests detailed information from FacultyProfiles.
8. FacultyProfiles provides detailed information to Researcher.
9. Researcher selects faculty members for collaboration.
10. Researcher saves/notes selected faculty members for collaboration in CollaborationModule.
11. CollaborationModule confirms the action and provides next steps to Researcher.
