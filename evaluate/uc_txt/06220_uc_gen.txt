1. The MRI Operator accesses the digital form interface.
2. The digital form interface displays the form to the MRI Operator.
3. The MRI Operator enters the subject responses in the form.
4. The form interface posts the responses to the Subject Responses API.
5. The Subject Responses API saves the responses in the Neurohub Database.
6. The Subject Responses API confirms the successful saving of responses in the database.
7. The form interface confirms the success to the MRI Operator by displaying a success message.
