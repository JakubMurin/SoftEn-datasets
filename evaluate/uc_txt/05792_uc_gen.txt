1. User requests to change marketing activity.
2. UMP activates.
3. UMP sends message to Retail-UMP.
4. Retail-UMP processes the change in marketing activity type.
5. Retail-UMP synchronously/asynchronously sends message to C.
6. C processes the message.
7. Retail-UMP returns.
