1. Patient registers at new practice.
2. NME updates registration GP with PDS.
3. PDS acknowledges update.
4. NME gets GP Connect record from Incumbent.
5. Incumbent provides GPC response to NME.
6. NME ingests FHIR data and saves new record.
7. NME sends acceptance request to NHAIS.
8. NHAIS approves the request with a business acknowledgment.
9. NHAIS deducts necessary information.
10. Incumbent acknowledges deduction to NHAIS.
