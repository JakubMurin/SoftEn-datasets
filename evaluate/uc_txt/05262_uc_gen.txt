1. Librarian navigates to BookPage.
2. Librarian clicks on Add Book.
3. BookPage displays Add Book Options Pop-up.
4. Librarian clicks on Add Book Option.
5. BookPage displays ISBN Input Popup.
6. Librarian enters ISBN.
7. BookPage requests Book Information by ISBN.
8. Controller retrieves Book Information by ISBN from OpenLibrary.
9. Controller adds Book to Database.
10. Controller responds with Addition Confirmation.
11. BookPage displays Addition Confirmation.
