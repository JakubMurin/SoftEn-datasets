1. Faculty Member enters credentials and requests authentication.
2. Credentials are validated by the Authentication Service.
3. Faculty Member gains access to the Search Interface.
4. Faculty Member inputs search criteria to find desired collection.
5. Database returns search results to Search Interface.
6. Search Interface displays list of matching collections.
7. Faculty Member selects a desired collection for further details.
8. Selected collection details are retrieved from the Database.
9. Faculty Member gains access to the Collection and views its content.
