1. Workshop Platform fetches request for summary.
2. CMS queries workshop data from the Database.
3. Database returns workshop data to CMS.
4. CMS generates summary content with Content Editor.
5. Content Editor submits the summary to CMS.
6. CMS updates the Database with the summary.
7. Database confirms the update to CMS.
8. CMS publishes the summary to the Summary Page.
9. Summary Page displays the summary content to Attendee.
