1. The Operator asks to see Job Openings.
2. The ListApplicationsUI creates the UI.
3. The ListApplicationsUI lists Job Openings.
4. The ListApplicationController finds all Job Openings in the JobOpeningRepository.
5. The JobOpeningRepository returns the list of Job Openings to the ListApplicationController.
6. The ListApplicationController provides the list of Job Openings to the UI.
7. The UI displays the Job Openings.
