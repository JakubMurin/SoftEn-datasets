1. The Buyer/Client sends a POST request for /faultManagementJob.
2. The Fault Management Job is created.
3. The Fault Management Job transitions to inProgress state.
4. Data is generated for the Fault Management Job.
5. The Fault Management Report is created.
6. The Fault Management Report transitions to inProgress state.
7. Data is collected for the Fault Management Report.
8. The Fault Management Report transitions to completed state.
9. The Fault Management Report content is returned to the Buyer/Client.
