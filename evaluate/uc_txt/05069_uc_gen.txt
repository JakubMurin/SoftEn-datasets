1. Apelant inserts the money.
2. Standard counts down for 2 minutes.
3. Apelant dials the number.
4. If the number is not dialed, Apelant hangs up.
5. If the number is dialed, Standard connects the line to Correspondant.
6. If the line is busy, Apelant hangs up.
7. If the line is free, Correspondant picks up.
8. Standard counts the money usage.
9. Apelant adds more money.
10. Apelant and Correspondant talk.
11. Correspondant hangs up.
12. Apelant hangs up.
13. Apelant and Correspondant disconnect the call.
14. Standard returns the money to Apelant.
