1. User selects the audio effect removal option.
2. Dropdown menu is activated displaying effect removal options.
3. Dropdown menu directs to close the effect removal panel.
4. Audio effect action triggers the removal of the selected effect.
5. Audio effect saga initiates the removal saga process.
6. Event emitter emits 'EFFECT_DELETE_EFFECT' event.
7. Audio controller removes the effect from the audio.
8. Dropdown menu removes the selected audio effect removal option.
