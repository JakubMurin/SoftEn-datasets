1. Buyer/Client sends POST /faultManagementJob request to initiate the process.
2. Fault Management Job is created by the system.
3. Fault Management Job is set to 'inProgress' state.
4. Data is generated by the system in the Fault Management Job.
5. A Fault Management Report is automatically triggered.
6. Fault Management Report is created by the system.
7. Fault Management Report is in 'inProgress' state and collecting data.
8. Fault Management Report is completed after data collection.
