2. Apelant dials the number.
3. If the number is not dialed, Apelant is prompted to hang up.
4. If the number is dialed, the call is connected to Correspondant.
5. In case the line is busy, Apelant hangs up.
6. If the line is free, Correspondant picks up the phone.
7. Money is deducted from the phone booth during conversation.
8. Apelant adds coins during the call.
9. Apelant and Correspondant communicate.
10. Correspondant hangs up the call.
11. Apelant hangs up the call.
12. Apelant and Correspondant disconnect.
13. Money is refunded back to Apelant.
