1. Health workers search for a patient.
2. User interface searches for a patient.
3. Engine searches for available CarePlan based on PlanDefinition.
4. Engine evaluates and creates CarePlan for valid PlanDefinitions.
5. Engine displays available CarePlan to user interface.
6. Health workers select a CarePlan.
7. User interface sends the selected CarePlan to Engine.
8. Engine saves the PlanDefinition for guideline-based care, diagnosis determination, medication dispensing, and counseling.
9. Engine creates an encounter.
10. Engine applies CarePlan and processes activities or questionnaires.
11. Engine determines diagnosis based on classification questionnaire.
12. Engine dispenses medications based on medication questionnaire.
13. Engine provides counseling based on counseling questionnaire.
