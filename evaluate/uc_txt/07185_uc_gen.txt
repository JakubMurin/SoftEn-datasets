1. Researcher logs in to the DMPTool.
2. Researcher navigates to the project DMP.
3. DMP fetches DMP record details.
4. Researcher selects "Add Publication".
5. Researcher enters publication details.
6. DMP checks if publication is in the Repo.
7. DMP links the publication with the DMP record.
8. Researcher requests a research output report.
9. DMP fetches all publications linked to DMP.
10. Repo returns publications data to DMP.
11. Report generates a report based on the publications.
12. DMP displays/downloads the report to the Researcher.
