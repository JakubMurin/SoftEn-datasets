1. Designer requests existing guides and standards from Team Member.
2. Team Member shares link to Guide Repository and CMS access with Designer.
3. Designer accesses the Guide Repository.
4. Guide Repository displays available guides (Style, Design, Content) to Designer.
5. Designer reviews and compiles relevant information from the guides.
6. Designer applies guides in the design process using CMS.
7. CMS confirms the application of guides by Designer.
8. Designer updates the Guide Repository with new/modified guides.
9. Guide Repository confirms the update to Designer.
10. Designer shares updated guides with Team Member.
11. Team Member reviews and provides feedback on the updated guides.
12. Team Member confirms the adoption of the updated guides to Designer.
