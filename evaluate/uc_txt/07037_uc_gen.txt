1. Researcher identifies criteria for interviewees.
2. Recruitment Tool queries potential participants in the database.
3. Database returns a list of potential interviewees to the Recruitment Tool.
4. Researcher reviews the list of potential participants.
5. Researcher selects potential interview participants.
6. Recruitment Tool sends invitations for interviews to selected participants.
7. Participants respond to the interview invitations.
8. Recruitment Tool reports responses to the Researcher.
9. Researcher schedules interviews with accepted participants.
10. Recruitment Tool confirms interview schedules with participants.
11. Participants confirm attendance for the interviews.
