1. Business User registers listeners for Fault Management notifications with the Hub.
2. Business User creates a Fault Management Job.
3. System Operator Framework creates the Fault Management Job.
4. System Operator Framework responds with the created Fault Management Job.
5. Fault Management Job is activated.
6. Fault Management Job generates necessary data.
7. Fault Management Job creates a Fault Management Report.
8. Fault Management Report is activated.
9. Fault Management Report collects data.
10. Fault Management Report completes data collection.
11. Fault Management Report is ready for delivery.
12. Business User requests the Fault Management Report.
13. System Operator Framework retrieves the Fault Management Report.
14. System Operator Framework responds with the retrieved Fault Management Report.
