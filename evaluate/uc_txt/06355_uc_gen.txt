1. The Buyer/Client initiates a POST request to create a Fault Management Job.
2. The Fault Management Job is successfully created.
3. The Fault Management Job progresses to the 'inProgress' state.
4. Data is generated for the Fault Management Job.
5. A Fault Management Report is created as a result.
6. The Fault Management Report progresses to the 'inProgress' state.
7. Data is collected for the Fault Management Report.
8. The Fault Management Report is completed.
9. The Fault Management Job is marked as completed.
10. The Buyer/Client requests the Fault Management Report.
11. The Fault Management Report content is provided to the Buyer/Client.
