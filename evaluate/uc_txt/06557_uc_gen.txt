1. User requests to get courses for a teacher by providing the teacher ID.
2. Controller retrieves the teacher information by ID.
3. Controller communicates with Teacher to get the teachings of the teacher.
4. Controller receives courses from Teacher.
5. Controller iterates over each course to gather course details.
6. Controller gets the ID of the course.
7. Controller gets the name of the course.
8. Controller gets the description of the course.
9. Controller gets the price of the course.
10. Controller gets the average rating of the course.
11. Controller gets the banner image URL of the course.
12. Controller sends course card data to the User.
