1. Inputter clicks on the "Court Details" link on the Account Details page.
2. Account Details page navigates to Court Details page.
3. Local Justice Areas service retrieves records from the Fines Database using JPA.
4. Local Justice Areas service returns Local Justice Areas records in JSON format.
5. Court Details page populates the Local Justice Areas auto-complete field.
6. Courts service retrieves records from the Fines Database using JPA.
7. Courts service returns Courts records in JSON format.
8. Court Details page populates the Courts auto-complete field.
9. Inputter enters Court details and clicks on "Return to account details" button.
10. Court Details page navigates back to Account Details page.
