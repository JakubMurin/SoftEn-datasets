1.  Step within main sequence .
2. Trainer accesses the platform.
3. Authentication system authenticates the Trainer successfully.
4. Trainer selects "My Trainings" on the dashboard.
5. Dashboard displays a list of Trainings.
6. Trainer selects the "Edit" option on the desired training node on the Edit Form.
7. Edit Form presents the Edit Form with training details.
8. Trainer modifies the training information and adds links to slides.
9. Edit Form displays the preview of the updated information.
10. Trainer submits the updated training information.
11. Edit Form updates the Training Node Information in the database.
12. Database confirms the update.
13. Edit Form displays the success message of the update.
