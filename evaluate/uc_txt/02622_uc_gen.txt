1. Frontend initiates map movement.
2. Frontend calculates the area.
3. Frontend adds the area to the queue.
4. Backend retrieves the depth in the area.
5. Backend requests points in the depth.
6. Points in the area are obtained.
7. Split Map and Split Queue exchange information.
8. Backend gets undefined points from Points database.
9. Backend calculates points.
10. Points are updated.
11. Points with undefined depth are inserted.
12. Backend creates overlay with current points.
13. Frontend shows the overlay to the User.
