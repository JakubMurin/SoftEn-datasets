1. User decides to pay with USDT.
2. Request payment information from Payment Gateway (USDT deposit address and amount).
3. Provides USDT deposit address and amount, including QR code.
4. Transfers USDT from User to Blockchain.
5. Detects transaction on Blockchain.
6. Request confirmation of transaction from Blockchain.
7. Updates transaction status based on 6 confirmations.
8. Notification of final transaction approval received.
9. Stores transaction and user information in Database.
10. Confirms payment and converts to points in Website UI.
