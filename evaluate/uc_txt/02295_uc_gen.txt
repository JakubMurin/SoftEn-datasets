1. Recruitment Coordinator checks existing templates in the Template Database.
2. If templates are not available, Database informs Recruitment Coordinator about non-availability.
3. Recruitment Coordinator requests specifics of the template from Team Member.
4. Team Member provides specifics to Recruitment Coordinator.
5. Recruitment Coordinator creates a draft of the template.
6. Recruitment Coordinator submits the draft to Approval Authority for approval.
7. Approval Authority reviews the template draft.
8. Approval Authority requests revisions if needed.
9. Recruitment Coordinator revises the template.
10. Recruitment Coordinator resubmits the revised template to Approval Authority for approval.
11. Approval Authority approves the template.
12. Recruitment Coordinator stores the new template in the Template Database.
13. Recruitment Coordinator informs Team Member about the availability of the template.
14. If templates are available, Database provides existing templates to Recruitment Coordinator.
15. Recruitment Coordinator shares the existing templates with Team Member.
