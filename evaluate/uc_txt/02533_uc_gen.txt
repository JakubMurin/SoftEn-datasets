1. Access the Menu Management section in the Management Interface.
2. Request the current menu items from the Backend Server.
3. Fetch the menu items data from the Database.
4. Display the menu items on the Management Interface.
5. Select the items to update.
6. Submit the updated menu items.
7. Update menu items request is sent to the Backend Server.
8. Update menu items in the Database.
9. Receive confirmation of the updated items from the Database.
10. Confirm successful update to the Manager.
11. Display a success message to the Manager.
