1. Designer requests existing guides and standards from Team Member.
2. Team Member shares the link to the Guide Repository and CMS access.
3. Designer accesses the repository and reviews the available guides (Style, Design, Content).
4. Designer compiles and reviews relevant information from the guides.
5. Designer applies the guides in the design process within the CMS.
6. CMS confirms the correct application of the guides by the Designer.
7. Designer updates the repository with new/modified guides.
8. Guide Repository confirms the successful update to the Designer.
9. Team Member reviews the updated guides and provides feedback.
10. Team Member confirms the adoption of the updated guides to the Designer.
