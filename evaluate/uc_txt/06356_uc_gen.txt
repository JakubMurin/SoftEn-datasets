1. Buyer/Client sends a POST request to create a Fault Management Job.
2. Fault Management Job is created by the system.
3. Fault Management Job progresses to the 'inProgress' state.
4. System generates data for the current job.
5. Fault Management Report is created as a result.
6. Fault Management Report progresses to the 'inProgress' state.
7. System collects data for the report.
8. Fault Management Report is completed.
9. Fault Management Job is marked as completed.
10. Buyer/Client requests to view the Fault Management Report.
11. Fault Management Report content is retrieved and displayed to the Buyer/Client.
