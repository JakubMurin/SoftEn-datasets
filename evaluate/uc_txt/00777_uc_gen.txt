1. Customer selects product and asks for a quote.
2. Shop receives request and selects "Crop Top".
3. CEIS fetches information on "Crop Top".
4. CEIS requests recipes for "Crop Top" from ProductCookbook.
5. ProductCookbook provides recipes for "Crop Top".
6. CEIS requests availability of input materials from MaterialFlowMonitor.
7. MaterialFlowMonitor provides options for each input material.
8. CEIS fetches costs and GHG impacts for each alternative from CostAnalyzer, WISER, and ERP.
9. CEIS adds alternatives to the list of possibilities and informs Shop.
10. Shop matches alternatives with company policies and presents them to the Customer.
11. Customer places the order.
12. CEIS informs ProcessControl about the order and deadlines.
13. ProcessControl schedules and monitors processes for order fulfillment.
14. ProcessControl informs MaterialFlowMonitor about results.
15. CEIS updates data in DigitalProductPassport.
