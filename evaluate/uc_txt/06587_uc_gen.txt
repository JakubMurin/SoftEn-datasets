1. Admin requests to view the list of advertisements.
2. List_advertises_view displays the list of advertisements.
3. Admin clicks on the edit button for an advertisement.
4. List_advertises_view redirects to edit_advertise_view.
5. Edit_advertise_view shows the content of the advertisement.
6. Admin edits the advertisement data and clicks the save button.
7. Edit_advertise_view submits the information.
8. AdsController validates the data.
9. AdsController saves the data.
10. AdsModel returns the saved data.
11. AdsController sends the result of saving the advertisement.
12. Edit_advertise_view displays the edited advertisement.
