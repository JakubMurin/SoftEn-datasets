1. The MRI Operator accesses the digital form interface.
2. The digital form interface displays the form to the MRI Operator.
3. The MRI Operator enters the subject responses into the form.
4. The MRI Operator submits the responses using the form interface.
5. The Subject Responses API receives and posts the responses.
6. The responses are saved in the Neurohub Database.
7. The Neurohub Database confirms successful saving of responses.
8. The Subject Responses API confirms the success of saving responses.
9. The digital form interface displays a success message to the MRI Operator.
