1. Admin accesses the Update Center Form through the UI.
2. UI displays the Update Center Form to the Admin.
3. Admin inputs the Recycling Center data into the form.
4. Admin submits the new Recycling Center information to the Server.
5. Server validates and adds the information to the Database.
6. Database confirms the successful addition of the information.
7. Server displays a success message to the UI.
8. UI shows a confirmation message to the Admin.
