1. User triggers the 'onAcceptButton' event in the HelloController.
2. HelloController creates a new instance of Bankomat.
3. HelloController initializes the Bankomat with selected bank, location, and initial balance.
4. HelloController adds a new UserAdmin with username 'admin' and PIN 5555 to the Bankomat.
5. HelloController sets the Bankomat instance in the SignIn_UpController.
6. SignIn_UpController displays the sign-in interface.
7. HelloController closes the sign-in process.
