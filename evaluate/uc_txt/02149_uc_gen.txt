1. Curator chooses 'Move Item'.
2. UI requests form to move item.
3. CMS displays form.
4. Curator submits form (Item ID, Source Collection, Target Collection).
5. UI validates and processes move request.
6. CMS fetches item from Source Collection.
7. Database retrieves Item Details.
8. CMS removes item from Source Collection.
9. CMS confirms removal.
10. CMS adds item to Target Collection.
11. CMS confirms addition.
12. UI displays success message.
13. UI confirms item moved to Curator.
