1. Admin selects A/V Quality Filter Option.
2. Admin Interface initiates A/V Quality Filtering.
3. Video Management System sends Video for A/V Analysis.
4. A/V Quality Assessment Module analyzes Video A/V Quality and stores A/V Quality Scores in Database.
5. A/V Analysis Complete.
6. Ranking Algorithm calculates Rankings based on A/V Quality and updates Rankings in Database.
7. Video Management System sends Filtered and Ranked Video List to Admin Interface.
8. Admin Interface displays Ranked Videos to Admin.
