1. Researcher identifies criteria for participants.
2. Recruitment Tool queries potential participants in the Database.
3. Database returns a list of potential participants to the Recruitment Tool.
4. Researcher reviews the list of potential participants.
5. Researcher selects potential interviewees.
6. Recruitment Tool sends invitations for interviews to Educators and Science Influencers.
7. Participants respond to the invitations.
8. Recruitment Tool reports responses (accept/decline) to the Researcher.
9. Researcher schedules interviews with acceptees.
10. Recruitment Tool confirms the interview schedule with the participants.
11. Participants confirm their attendance.
12. Recruitment Tool finalizes the list of interview participants.
