1. OtherWeb requests login to AuthApp with app-key=dapp.
2. AuthApp fetches app info from AuthServer.
3. AuthApp connects to Nami.
4. AuthApp fetches random number from AuthServer.
5. AuthApp requests Nami signature.
6. Nami provides signature to AuthApp.
7. AuthApp logs in to AuthServer with signature.
8. AuthApp redirects to OtherWeb with access token.
