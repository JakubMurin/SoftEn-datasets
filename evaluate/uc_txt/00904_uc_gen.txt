1. Student takes an exam.
2. List of unsolved automatic exams is retrieved.
3. List of automatic exams is displayed to the Student.
4. Student selects an automatic exam to take.
5. Exam is built for the Student to take.
6. Student makes and answers questions.
7. Questions are validated.
8. Exam grade is calculated in percentage.
9. Grades are displayed to the Student.
10. Grade is saved for the Student.
11. Feedback is shown to the Student.
