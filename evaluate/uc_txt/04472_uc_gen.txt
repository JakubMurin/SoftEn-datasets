1. Apotheker registers for AZ.
2. AVS registers with Apothekendienstleister.
3. AVS provides URLs.
4. AVS generates dataset.
5. AVS signs dataset with C.HCI.OSIG.
6. AVS uploads signed dataset to APOVZD Upload-Container.
7. APOVZD Upload-Container verifies signature of dataset.
8. APOVZD Upload-Container stores dataset for synchronization.
9. APOVZD syncs entries with APOVZD.
10. APOVZD gets certificates from VZD.
11. APOVZD updates pharmacy with URLs and C.HCI.ENC-certificates.
