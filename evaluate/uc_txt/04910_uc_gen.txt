1. User Researcher searches for potential participants based on interview topic.
2. User Research Management System queries for staff with relevant expertise from NSF Staff Database.
3. NSF Staff Database returns a list of potential NSF participants to User Research Management System.
4. User Research Management System presents potential participants to User Researcher.
5. User Researcher selects NSF participants for interviews.
6. User Research Management System sends invitation emails with interview details via Email System.
7. Email System receives the interview invitation.
8. NSF Person RSVPs to the invitation.
9. Email System logs the RSVP status.
10. User Research Management System confirms NSF participant RSVPs.
