1. Guest clicks on the link in the email.
2. Mail provides the token to Page1.
3. Page1 checks the token with API1 for guild registration.
4. API1 retrieves the existing token from Guest_Token.
5. API1 sends the token to Guest_Token for validation.
6. API1 retrieves the pre-registered guild from Guild.
7. Guild provides the guild name to API1.
8. API1 sends the guild ID, guild name, and email address to Page1.
