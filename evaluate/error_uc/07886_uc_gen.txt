1. Buyer registers itself as an observer with the Market.
2. (Loop): Seller puts item up for sale.
3. Market notifies the Buyer of the new items available.
4. (Option loop): Buyer registers itself as an observer after Market has items on sale.
5. Market notifies the Buyer of the new items available.
6. First condition passed: Buyer has money to buy the item.
7. Buyer buys the item from the Seller.
8. Seller removes the item from the market.
9. Buyer receives the item from the Seller.
10. Buyer has money to buy the item.
11. Buyer buys the item from the Seller.
12. Seller removes the item from the market.
13. Buyer receives the item from the Seller.
